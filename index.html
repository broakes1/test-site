The Institute of Queer Ecology
The Institute of Queer Ecology is a non-profit organization dedicated to bringing queer discourse to ecology.
Our mission is to use biology and evolution to contextualize non-normative sexualities, adaptations and presentations.
Our programming revolves around migratory exhibitions, online publications, and a yearly journal.

We are always looking to collaborate with scientists, artists, queers, theorists,
writers, designers, people, plants, and animals. Interested in working with us?
Email info@queerecology.com, and check back with this site as we continue to build it over the next few months.
